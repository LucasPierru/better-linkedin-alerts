# Better LinkedIn Alerts

A filtering system for LinkedIn job postings that helps you find relevant opportunities based on specific criteria.

## Features

- **Keyword Filtering**: Filter jobs based on keywords found in job descriptions
- **Location Filtering**: Target specific locations or geographic areas
- **Work Mode Filtering**: Filter by work arrangement (remote, hybrid, on-site)

## How It Works

This project enhances your job search experience by automatically filtering LinkedIn job postings according to your preferences, helping you focus on the most relevant opportunities without manually browsing through hundreds of listings.
